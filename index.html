History

HarmonyTone Music Sdn. Bhd. was established in 2010 by a group of passionate musicians and guitar lovers in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Originally starting as a small independent guitar shop, the company has grown into one of the most recognized retailers and distributors of guitars and music accessories in Southeast Asia.


Driven by the belief that music is a universal language and a lifelong hobby, HarmonyTone focuses on curating the best selection of acoustic, electric, and classical guitars to inspire both beginners and professional musicians alike. The store's founders envisioned a place where anyone—from school students to touring artists—could find not only quality instruments but also community, support, and education.


The brand’s motto, “Strum Your Story,” reflects its commitment to helping musicians express themselves through the strings of a guitar. Today, HarmonyTone also offers workshops, jam sessions, and guitar maintenance services, and supports local music festivals and school music programs across Malaysia.
